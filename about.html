About us

about our Factory & industries

Transport Engineering Solutions India (TESI) was founded in January 2006 in Chakan MIDC, Pune. We are a premier manufacturer of comprehensive road transport solutions, operating a state-of-the-art facility with advanced engineering and design capabilities.

Vision & Mission

Our Motto

International quality, reliable service, and complete customer orientation.

Vision

To be recognized as a truly global provider of complete road transportation solutions, introducing safer, innovative, and higher-quality technologies to the Indian market through global collaborations.

Mission

To design and manufacture Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) that satisfy specific customer requirements regarding cost, quality, performance, and lifecycle.

Growth Strategy

TESI is aggressively expanding its market share with OEMs, institutional buyers, and fleet operators in India, while simultaneously targeting export markets.

Engineering Action. Delivering Performanc e. Driving Progress.

At TESI, engineering ideas are transformed into robust transport solutions. With advanced manufacturing practices and global collaborations,
we deliver products that meet international standards. Our focus remains on reliability, efficiency, and long-term value for every customer we serve.
