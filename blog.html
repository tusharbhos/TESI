Engineering-Manufacturing Excellence

Engineering Excellence That Delivers Performance At Transport Engineering Solutions India (TESI), engineering forms the backbone of every solution we deliver. Our in-house engineering and design teams work closely with manufacturing to develop robust, application-specific transport solutions that meet performance, safety, and regulatory requirements. TESI operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility at Chakan MIDC, Pune, equipped with modern fabrication, assembly, and finishing infrastructure. Through standardized processes, precision fixturing, and strict quality controls, we ensure consistency, durability, and reliability across all product lines. Our engineering-driven approach enables TESI to deliver international-quality products while remaining optimized for Indian operating conditions and lifecycle costs.

Comprehensive Transport Solutions

Complete Solutions for Road Transportation TESI offers a comprehensive portfolio of transport engineering solutions designed for logistics, infrastructure, mining, automotive, and industrial transportation applications. Our product range includes rigid tippers, semi-trailers, tip trailers, curtainsiders, and other customized Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs). All solutions are engineered to integrate seamlessly with major OEM chassis platforms such as Tata Motors, Mahindra, Ashok Leyland, Eicher, Volvo, BharatBenz, EKA Mobility, and Blue Energy. TESI works closely with customers to understand operational requirements and deliver solutions that optimize payload efficiency, safety, and long-term performance. Both standard and custom-engineered solutions are developed with a focus on compliance, reliability, and total cost of ownership.

Global Partnerships. Local Expertise.

International Collaboration, Indian Manufacturing TESI’s strategic joint ventures with global industry leaders enhance its technical capabilities and product offerings. Through collaborations with Lohr Industries (France) and Mosolf Group (Germany), TESI brings global technology, proven designs, and best manufacturing practices to the Indian market. These partnerships enable TESI to deliver advanced solutions for car and truck carriers, finished vehicle logistics, and specialized transportation applications, while maintaining local manufacturing efficiency and responsiveness. By combining international expertise with deep understanding of Indian logistics requirements, TESI delivers solutions that meet global standards and local performance expectations.
